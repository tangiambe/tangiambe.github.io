About Me

Hello World!

My name is Tangi Ambe. I'm a data engineer based in the Washington, DC Metro Area with a goal-driven mindset and passion for continous learning and innovating!


Interestingly, I first got introduced to programming during my senior year of high school when I took an Intro to Web and Mobile Development class.
